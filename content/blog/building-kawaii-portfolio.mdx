## The Kawaii Design System

### Color Palette
- **Primary Pink**: `#ec4899` - Warm, inviting, and professional
- **Secondary Purple**: `#8b5cf6` - Adds depth and sophistication
- **Accent Rose**: `#f43f5e` - For highlights and call-to-actions

### Typography Hierarchy
- **Headings**: Playfair Display - Elegant and readable
- **Body**: Poppins - Modern and accessible
- **Code**: Geist Mono - Clean and developer-friendly

### Animation Principles
1. **Easing**: Always use `easeOut` for natural movement
2. **Duration**: Keep animations under 600ms
3. **Stagger**: Animate children with slight delays
4. **Physics**: Use spring animations for organic feel
